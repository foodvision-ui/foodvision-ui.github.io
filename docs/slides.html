The solution
04 · FoodVision AI

Three moments, every time you cook.

STEP 01

Photo in, recipe out

Qwen3-VL-32B reads your fridge. You get a dish, prep steps, and nutrition tailored to your current profile.

STEP 02

Memory

Last 14 days of meals feed into the next prompt. The AI stops repeating and starts noticing patterns.

STEP 03

Quiet learning

Cooked Korean three times? Korean appears in your profile. You never typed it.

07 · Under the hood

The learning loop.

"Here's the profile. Here are their recent meals.
Suggest additive updates based on patterns."
SAFETY

Additive-only

The AI can add to a user's preferences. It can never delete what the user set.

HONESTY

Provenance tracking

Every learned field is tagged. The Profile page shows which preferences came from you AI learned vs. from the AI.

Recap
10 · What you just saw

What was hard.

ISSUE 01

Cross-request visibility

Jac writes in one request weren't visible to queries in the next. Fix: pass the data explicitly instead of querying the graph.

ISSUE 02

AI output reliability

Qwen3-VL sometimes emits broken JSON — trailing commas, <think> tags. Fix: a four-layer fallback parser.

ISSUE 03

The overwrite bug

First merge clobbered existing preferences. Caught in code review, not QA. Fix: case-insensitive union-dedupe.
